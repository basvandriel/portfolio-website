## üéì KEY LEARNINGS & PRINCIPLES

### What Makes B2B Consulting Sites Convert

1. **Specificity Beats Generality**
   - "‚Ç¨500K wasted" > "Save money"
   - "90 days" > "Fast results"
   - "80% failure reduction" > "More reliable"

2. **Differentiation is Everything**
   - Don't compete on features (everyone has them)
   - Compete on positioning ("vs. agencies")
   - Emphasize what makes you unique

3. **Multiple Conversion Paths**
   - Not everyone is ready to book a call
   - Offer low-commitment options (lead magnet)
   - Nurture cold leads into warm prospects

4. **Urgency Without Sleaze**
   - Real scarcity: "Only 2 spots left" (if true)
   - Seasonal urgency: "Q4 filling up"
   - Social proof: "247 downloads this month"

5. **Trust is Built in Layers**
   - Layer 1: Enterprise logos
   - Layer 2: Specific metrics
   - Layer 3: Detailed case studies
   - Layer 4: Video testimonials
